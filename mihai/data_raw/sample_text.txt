Event: Bucharest Sunday Hikers Group
Category: Sports & Outdoors
Description: Join us for a beginner-friendly hike in the Comana Forest. This is a great way to escape the city noise and meet new people. We walk at a slow pace, allowing plenty of time for conversation and photos. After the hike, we usually stop at a local restaurant for lunch.
Target Audience: Nature lovers, people looking for a relaxed group.
Date: Every Sunday at 09:00 AM.
Location: Meeting point is Piata Unirii Subway Station, Bucharest.
Cost: Free (share gas money if carpooling).
Contact: hikers@example.com
